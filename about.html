About Me

Fullstack Rookie

My name is Ronny Bruhin and I am a Fullstack Developer Rookie. I started my apprenticeship in August 2023 and really enjoy it. Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.

Outside of work, I find joy in a variety of fulfilling hobbies. You might find me taking leisurely walks with my dogs, savoring the tranquility of nature. At home, I indulge in nurturing my passion for technology by tinkering with my homeserver setup, ensuring everything runs smoothly. The art of bonsai captivates me, offering a blend of patience and creativity as I meticulously care for miniature trees. Gardening in all its forms brings me contentment; whether it's cultivating vibrant blooms or growing fresh produce, I revel in nurturing life from seed to fruition. Additionally, I delve into the world of 3D printing, exploring its limitless possibilities and bringing digital designs to tangible reality. These pursuits not only enrich my downtime but also reflect my diverse interests and dedication to continuous learning and creativity.

After nearly 15 years in construction, specializing as a plumber and heating system installer, I've finally stepped into the role I've always aspired to. Transitioning into this new chapter of my career has been a rewarding journey, filled with the satisfaction of pursuing my passion. Now, I'm fully immersed in software development, where every day brings new opportunities to thrive and grow professionally. This career change not only represents a culmination of years of hard work and dedication but also signifies a fulfilling leap into a role that truly resonates with my ambitions and interests.
